About US


"Course Correct" is a cutting-edge web application tailored to meet the diverse academic needs of students at USIU-Africa. Serving as a centralized hub, this platform offers a comprehensive catalog of previous exam papers across various majors. With an intuitive user interface, the application presents a visually appealing gallery of courses on its homepage. Each course tile is interactive, leading users to dedicated pages featuring concise product descriptions and easily accessible download links for related exam papers. In future updates, Course Correct aims to introduce a premium feature, allowing users to access solutions for exam papers through a subscription model, thereby enhancing exam preparation. The application is set to incorporate M-Pesa integration for seamless and secure payment transactions. Moreover, Course Correct boasts a dynamic search bar functionality, enabling users to efficiently locate specific courses or majors of interest, thereby minimizing navigation time. Through continuous refinement and user-centric design, Course Correct aspires to be the primary platform for USIU-Africa students, offering valuable academic resources and a sustainable business model.
